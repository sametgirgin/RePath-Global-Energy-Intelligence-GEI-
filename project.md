### A Comprehensive Intelligence Platform for Global Energy Statistics

**RePath Global Energy Intelligence (GEI)** is an advanced statistical and analytics platform developed by **RePath Analytics** to deliver a unified, transparent, and data-rich view of the world’s energy systems.  
Designed for researchers, policymakers, financial institutions, and energy professionals, the Atlas consolidates decades of global data into a single, coherent framework—transforming complex datasets into actionable strategic insight.

The platform integrates historical and contemporary datasets across the full energy value chain, including oil, gas, coal, renewables, electricity markets, critical minerals, hydrogen, emissions, and energy transition indicators.  
Each dataset is standardized, validated, and structured for powerful comparative analysis, long-term modelling, and evidence-based decision-making.

Backed by RePath Analytics’ expertise in energy modelling, data engineering, and market intelligence, the **Global Energy Intelligence (GEI)** serves as both a reference library and an analytical engine.  
Its purpose is simple yet ambitious:  
**to provide the most reliable, accessible, and comprehensive view of global energy flows and market fundamentals available today.**

Whether you are analysing historical supply trends, benchmarking energy transition metrics, or forecasting future system pathways, the RePath Global Energy Intelligence (GEI) is designed to be your central, trusted source of energy knowledge.
